About

Lookdev Artist / Lighting Artist / Illustrator

Hey there! I'm Darren Hing — artist, toolmaker, and professional button masher. By day, I wrangle nodes and shaders in the film industry, with over a decade in feature animation working on projects including The Highway Rat, Revolting Rhymes, and more. By night I swap the techy hat for a sketchpad, conjuring characters and the worlds they live in. I build cool things, break them, then build them better.
